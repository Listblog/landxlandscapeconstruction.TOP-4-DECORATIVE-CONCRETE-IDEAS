STAMPED CONCRETE

Poured concrete can look to plain if you leave it untreated; however, it's possible to give your concrete driveways, pathways, walkways, patios and deck areas a unique and interesting look by stamping them in different designs. You also have the option to choose the color of concrete you like and then get it stamped in the design of your choice. Some designs emulate the look of natural stone and brick too.

STENCILED CONCRETE

One way to lend a unique look to your landscape is to use common materials in a unique way to create the ambiance and appearance you want. If you feel that concrete surfaces like driveways, entranceways, pathways and patios or even poolscapes look very boring, you should consider stenciled concrete as an option. Using colored or stained concrete installations and then stenciling them is a great way to add the interest qoutient to your landscape.

COLORED CONCRETE

Every homeowner wants their landscape to look interesting and attractive and the one way to spruce up the look of your yard and garden spaces is to opt for colored concrete. These surfaces are stunning in every way and you can choose from a variety of colors and hues such as red, brown, green, blue, grey, or any other color you like. These colors are incorporated into the mix itself and you don't have to worry about them fading out. You can choose colors that complement the look of your landscape and home.

STAINED CONCRETE

Staining concrete is one more way of altering the look of the masonry feature. There are a number of different colored stains to choose from. In addition to staining the concrete, you also have the option to get it polished to give it a very attractive sheen. These surfaces work well in open-to-the-air spaces such as driveways, patios and decks as well as semi-enclosed or enclosed areas on your property.
